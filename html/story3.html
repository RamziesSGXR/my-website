As Tommy's friends were about to give up on unlocking the box, a magical creature named Spark appeared before them. Spark told them that the box contained the power to grant one wish but warned that there would be consequences depending on their choice. Spark then presented Tommy with three keys, each corresponding to a different ending.

Choose one of the three keys to unlock the box:

  • Ending A: Key of Wealth
  • Ending B: Key of Knowledge
  • Ending C: Key of Adventure
